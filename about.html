ABOUT

A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

Make it available the right practices that can be followed by the local farmer community, using mobile and internet. SMARTi as a company building these locale specific & crop specific community of Practices for the farmers, that are scientifically driven yet put it in a simplest way to be followed by farmer using the resource available at his disposal. For eg.15 Liters of Spray Drum is available for a small farmer that can be used to address multiple requirements. Farmers who are generally devoid of information and critical education, training needs knowledge of Market place etc... Trying to remove these INFORMATION barriers and make it available to the grass root level leveraging the technology. Bringing them into the overall supply chain as an integral part of the society and thus sharing the benefits available in the todays’ connected world.


OBJECTIVES

  • Enrich the qualities and availability of Vitamins and Protein’s in the Food – essential for healthy life.
  • To bring about the next phase of revolution in Organic Cultivation using SMARTi and Information Technologies available to farmers on his phone.
  • Easy access to latest technologies, best cultivation process and methods to the farmers in India.
  • Facilitate the soil-conditioning and all around growth of crops organically from “Roots to Fruits”
  • Bring the natural organic aroma, taste and shelf-life to the farm produce.
  • Enable Urban farming is an easy and satisfying experience through Balconies, Terrace & Home Garden.
  • All of these plus building inborn immunity of plants using smart and biologically balanced formulation called SMARTi
