HOW TO USE

Organic Micro-Nutrients Solution and Soil Conditioner.

Containing rare mineral, metals, chlorophyll, organic matters, Humic Acid, Fulvic acid and other key Amino Acids in a balanced formulation as required biologically for the overall growth of plants. A perfect enabler for the ORGANIC production of food and vegetables. Essentially used for high yields, enhancing the natural organic qualities Taste and Aroma of fruits and vegetables, Re-conditioning soil, stronger growth of roots and leaves, Providing boost to cultivate organically. The power of oceanic elements combined with earthen elements helps improvements in and around the fields, and water bodies.

How to Use:

Used by diluting in water as below dilution: 200 times for flower gardens, 500 times for vegetable, 1000 times for field crops. For Fruits plants 5 to 25 ml in 500 times water depending on the plant variety, size and age Approx. 200ml Per Acer is Used for Field Crop Soil Application. And for best results approx. 200 ml Per Acer is used for Leaves and Root using drenching method during early growth stage.
